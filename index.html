About Me

Fibula, also known as Barash, is a psychedelic music producer based in the north of Israel, in Haifa.

Falling in love with electronic music was a one-way ticket. That sound gave me a spark, and I try to bring that same energy to every track I make.

Join me on a journey of unique sound and experience — weird, unexpected, and worth it.
